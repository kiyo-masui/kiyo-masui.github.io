I am a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of British Columbia. My research interests lie in cosmology where I work on theory, data analysis, and observations. Cosmology (a division of astrophysics), is the study of the evolution of the Universe on the largest scales. More specifically I work on the large-scale structure of the Universe and observations of the structure at radio wavelengths.
